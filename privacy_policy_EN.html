This Privacy Policy explains how the Joyglo mobile application (the "App") collects, uses and protects your personal data, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — EU Regulation 2016/679) and applicable French data protection law.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is:

Mickaël BOURGIS 106 av de Strasbourg, 57070 METZ, France Contact: kaiserwallas@proton.me

2. Data We Collect

When you use the App, we collect and process the following data:

  • Account data: email address, username, password (stored encrypted, never in plain text).
  • Profile data: profile picture (avatar), if you add one.
  • Published content: the text and images of your posts, and your comments.
  • Interaction data: your likes, the accounts you follow, the reports you submit.
  • Notifications: events concerning you (likes, comments, new followers).

We do not collect precise location data or payment data.

3. Purposes of Processing

Your data is used to:

  • create and manage your user account;
  • allow you to publish, view and interact with content;
  • display your public profile (username, avatar, posts) to other users;
  • send you notifications about your activity;
  • ensure the security of the App and content moderation.

4. Legal Basis

Processing of your data is based on:

  • performance of the contract (the Terms of Use) you accept when creating an account;
  • your consent for the content you choose to publish;
  • our legitimate interest in ensuring the security and proper functioning of the service.

5. Hosting and Processors

App data is hosted via Supabase, in a region located within the European Union. Our technical providers act as processors within the meaning of the GDPR and are bound by confidentiality and security obligations.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and content are deleted within a reasonable period, unless a legal retention obligation applies.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • right of access to your data;
  • right to rectification of inaccurate data;
  • right to erasure ("right to be forgotten");
  • right to restriction of processing;
  • right to data portability;
  • right to object to processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: kaiserwallas@proton.me.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority, the CNIL (www.cnil.fr).

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data: password encryption, database-level access control, and secure connections.

9. Minors

The App is not intended for persons under the age of 15. If you are a minor, you must obtain the consent of your legal guardian before using the App.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Any significant change will be notified within the App. The date of the last update appears at the top of this document.

11. Contact
